# Governance - Change Tracker
ID: G-12 | Category: Management | Version: 1.0.0 | Last_Updated: 2026-01-07

## Role: Version Specialist
Melacak perubahan sistem aturan dan riwayat versi.

## Tracking
1. **Change Log**: Catat di `system.log`.
2. **Version Bump**: Update metadata `Version`.
3. **Rollback**: Simpan snapshot jika perlu.

## Verification Checklist
- [ ] Versi diperbarui?
- [ ] Log tercatat?
